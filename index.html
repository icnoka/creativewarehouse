About Us

Creative Warehouse is a Product, Industrial, Graphic designing and Screen-Printing company which has the ability and skills to provide Quality Service. Our uniqueness is in our willingness to take on new challenges

Creative Warehouse offers 3D Design Services including: 3D Product and industrial design, Product Animation, Interior Design Conceptualization including Offices/Shop Set ups

Creative Warehouse also partners with ICAM SOLUTIONS, an IT firm that is into developing and maintaining custom made applications such as Website designing, Mobile Apps, USSD and desktop applications for clients.

Our Team

Creative Warehouse's team of dedicated professionals together with trusted partners all across the country, work seamlessly to deliver the best Design, Fabrication and Application Development Solutions.

With a service philosophy of 60% design and 40% implementation plus a close working relationship with clients, we ensure that all designs are continually assessed and tested prior to implementation.

These efforts enable the efficient and timely implementation of projects, with minimal disruption of the client's daily operation during installation. Our team is dedicated to providing the best solutions as our core focus is on customer satisfaction driven by our passion for excellence
